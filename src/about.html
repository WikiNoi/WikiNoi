[[WikiNoi|noi/wikinoi/name]] is a TiddlyWiki-inspired non-linear wiki engine with a focus on simplicity and use of modern Web standards (even if some of them are [[not universally implemented|noi/wikinoi/browsers]]). WikiNoi is a subtle and respectful reference to TiddlyWiki. One of the meanings of the word _tiddly_ is [_little, tiny_](https://en.oxforddictionaries.com/definition/tiddly#h69933686572040). **noi** (น้อย) means _small_ in Thai. Why Thai? _"Because my fiancée is Thai"_ [[@yrashk|noi/wikinoi/contributors/yrashk]] It has also [been reported](https://twitter.com/BeaBonobo/status/1055886847275163648) that _noi_ means _us/we_ in Italian, so an Italian speaker could read it as "OurWiki".
